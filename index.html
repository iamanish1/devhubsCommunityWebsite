About DevHubs.in

DevHubs.in is a developer-centric community focused on bridging the skill and experience gap between students and the industry. We provide hands-on learning experiences through free workshops, premium mentorship programs, and real-world project opportunities.

Our mission is to empower the next generation of developers with practical skills, industry insights, and a supportive community to accelerate their career growth.
